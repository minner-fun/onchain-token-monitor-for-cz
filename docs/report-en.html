TL;DR

This is not even a standalone project. CZ was launched through four.meme (a public BSC token launchpad; the …4444 vanity suffix is its default), and upstream sits a Binance-fed "BNB faucet" 0xe2fc… that received ~$82M from Binance and fanned it out, one-way, to 63,602 addresses — shared plumbing used by a whole population of token deployers. CZ is one pool that an operator picked out of this public infrastructure and pumped. The genuinely private, coordinated operation is small: CZ's deployer + a 70% cold wallet + a wash-trading fleet.

The CZ pool itself: within 51 seconds of launch, one address locked 70% of total supply (700M tokens) into a cold wallet that has never moved a single token since. Free chips + fake volume: all the downside is on the buy side, under a 70% guillotine.
